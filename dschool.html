the project

Conceptualize and prototype a tool to teach design thinking to anyone in the world.

challenge

The Design School organized the hackathon (Hack.d) as a way to draw inspiration from its community for the direction it's website. Our team developed a product that had the potential for global impact the D.school stakeholders sought. We wanted to come up with the ideal design thinking tool.

solution

We applied the design thinking process to arrive at our final product, d.canvas - a platform that guides users through design thinking and made the results transparent for anyone in the world to discover, iterate on and learn from. Our team was selected one of two winning teams by a panel of judges - that included the founders of both IDEO and Mozilla. Such an honor.
