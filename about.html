About

With over 11 years as a designer, I enjoy working with small cross-functional teams to envision, design, and implement meaningful user experiences. As a UX practitioner, wearing the hat of user researcher, interaction designer, and visual designer, I always strive for the best possible experience for our customers.

I currently work for Planetary. Prior to that, I headed up design at my own startup, and worked with others including Skillshare and Airbnb. While working on small to mid-sized teams, I've had a chance to influence the product in a variety of cultures.

When I'm not at work, you can find me taking photos of textures and botanicals, going hiking, or volunteering for open source projects.
