the project

Construct a tool to help city-dwellers disrupt urban inertia.

challenge

In an increasingly urban-focused world, it's becoming more and more challenging for people to get time outdoors. Despite the density of the city, people can feel isolated and often lack an accessible social alternative to bars or parties as places to build connections with new and existing friends.

solution

The Hikery is a way to strike a more ideal balance between urban living and getting time outdoors by using a social platform for joining and creating hike experiences. The product generalizes the online/offline process of going hiking, facilitates the interactions involved and helps people to build meaningful, new relationships as a result.
